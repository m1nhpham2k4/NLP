## 1. Tam nhim

Tro thanh Truong dai hoc hang dau cua Viet Nam, tien phong trong linh vuc giao duc va nghien curu urng dung, chuyen giao cong nghe va doi moi sang tao:

- Tro thanh Truong dai hoc hang dau cua Viet Nam: dung trong top 10 cac truong dai hoc trong nuoc;
- Tien phong trong linh vyc giao duc va nghien ciu ung dung: cac chuong trinh dao tao dugc tiep can theo huong ung dung, dap ung dugc yeu cau thuc tien cua xa hoi;
- Chuyen giao cong nghe va doi moi sang tao: phat trien y tuong moi, cai tien, sang mai hoa san pham khoa hoc.

## 2. Sur mang

Cung cap nguon nhan luc chat luong cao, co nang luc, pham chat va hoi nhap quoc onu jep eno lou ex gi quy un ieud ns ouo dos suop ueu gi

- Nguon nhan luc chat lurong cao: co trinh do chuyen mon dat chuan, dap ung yeu cau thuc tien cua xa hoi, co nang lurc tur duy sang tao va nang luc khoi nghiep;
- Co nang luc, pham chat: co dao durc, co long nhan ai; luon co tinh than dam me, nghien curu, hoc hoi; co tu duy logic chat che, co sang kién dot pha, lam viec an toan, co tinh than hop tac; co thai do, tac phong lam viec tot, trach nhiem voi cong viec;
- Hoi nhap quoc te: co kha nang thich ung trong moi truong quoc té; c6 kién thuc ngoai ngu va cong nghe thong tin de tiép can nhanh tri thuc, cong nghe hien dai trong khu vuc va the gioi;
- Dong gop cho su phat trien cua dat nuoc: nguon nhan lurc chat lurong cao se lam tang nang suat lao dong, dap ung yeu cau cua thi truong trong nuroc va quoc te; lam giau cho doi song tri thurc va kinh te cung nhu hoat dong nghe nghiep, thong qua cac dich vu cong dong va dong gop vao su phat trien kinh te xa hoi cua dat nuoc.

## 3. Muc tieu chung

Xay dung cong nghe quan ly hien dai, hoan thanh co ban cac dieu kien dap ung yeu cau dao tao nguon nhan lurc chat lurong cao dong gop cho xa hoi, tao ra gia tri thyc tien va hieu qua tir hoat dong nghien curu khoa hoc va chuyen giao cong nghe, hop tac quoc te;

<!-- image -->

Tien phong trong cac linh vyc giao duc, nghien curu va chuyen giao cong nghe; thuc day ket noi phuc vu cong dong; tao moi truong hoc tap va lam viec thu hut nguoi hoc va giang vien co nang lyrc va trinh do cao; phat trien ben vung tren co so ty chu tai chinh.

De dat dugc muc tieu chung, Truong Dai hoc Cong nghiep Thanh ph6 Ho Chi Minh da dat ra cac muc tieu nhu sau:

- Tien phong trong cac linh vrc giao duc, nghien curu va chuyen giao cong nghe: tang chat lurong nguoi hoc tot nghiep; nang cao ty le co viec lam va vi tri viec lam cho nguoi hoc sau khi tot nghiep; tang cuong quoc té hoa cac chuong trinh dao tao; nang cao chat luong chuong trinh dao tao de dap ung yeu cau cua cac to churc dam bao chat lurong; tang cuong hop tac voi cac to churc trong viec tuyen dung nguoi hoc tot nghiep; tang chat luong cac cong trinh nghien curu cong bo trong nuoc va quoc te; thuc thi cac chinh sach ho tro hop tac nghien curu trong nuoc va quoc té; thuc day hoat dong chuyen giao cong nghe;
- Thuc day ket noi phuc vu cong dong: mo rong cac hoat dong cua cong dan tich cuc trong két noi cong dong; day manh cac hoat dong khoi nghiep; khuyén khich nguoi hoc, vien churc va nguoi lao dong tham gia cac hoat dong phuc vu cong dong; khai thac cac phuong tien truyen thong mot cach hieu qua; phat trien nang luc doi ngu lam truyen thong;
- Xay dung co so giao duc hien dai: phat trien co so ha tang cho hoat dong giang day va nghien curu; tao ra nhieu hoat dong ngoai khoa soi dong ngay trong khuon vien Nha truong; xay dung khuon vien xanh va co so ha tang cong nghe thong tin tien tién; phat trien he thong hoc tap tryc tuyen hieu qua;
- Thu hut nguon luyc chat lurong cao trong va ngoai nuoc: duy tri dugc doi ngu giang vien co nang lurc; co co che dai ngo phu hop; tuyen dung va tim kiem nhan su chat lurong cao; da dang hoa hinh thurc tuyen dung; tang curong phat trién nang luc nhan sy; thuc hien b6 tri cong viec phu hop; cai thien moi truong lam viec; nang cao thu nhap cua vien churc, nguroi lao dong;
- Dau tur va thu nhap on dinh, ben vung: tang doanh thu thong qua cac hoat dong dao tao, nghien curu va cac tai nguyen khac; dau tu hop ly cho nguon nhan lurc, dao tao va nghien curu, hoat dong phuc vu cong dong va nang cao chat lurong cuoc song nguoi hoc; duy tri khoan du phong; tang cuong ngan sach cho ke hoach dam bao hoat dong lien tuc.

## 4. Gia tri cot loi

Voi muc tieu va tam nhin tro thanh Truong dai hoc hang dau cua Viet Nam, tien phong trong linh vyc giao duc va nghien curu ung dung, chuyen giao cong nghe va doi moi sang tao; gia tri cot loi Truong Dai hoc Cong nghiep Thanh ph6 Ho Chi Minh dugc duc ket: "Doi moi - Doan ket - Nhan van".

- Doi moi: lien tuc tim kiem nhung cach lam moi va tot hon, sang tao, doi moi va dam nghi, dam lam de thur nhung dieu moi va tao co hoi de cai tién va dat den tam cao moi;

<!-- image -->

- Doan ket: chiu trach nhiem va lam chu, dong thoi phoi hop lam viec voi tat ca cac don vi va cac ben lien quan de khai thac triet de cac nguon lyrc va nang lyc cuing nhu sur dung va trien khai hieu qua chung;
- Nhan van: doi xir voi tung ca nhan va tap the bang long nhan ai, vi tha, cong bang, cong tam va huong dén phat trien tiem nang va hien thuc hoa nguyen vong; lam viéc chuyen nghiep va dao duc cuing nhu phuc vu va dong gop cho cong dong voi tur cach la mot cong dan c6 trach nhiem.

## 5. Phuong cham hoat dong

"Doi moi tur duy, lam giau them tri thurc - doi song" la phuong cham hoat dong cua Trurong Dai hoc Cong nghiep Thanh ph6 Ho Chi Minh nham dat dugc muc tieu, tam nhin va sur mang cua Nha truong.
